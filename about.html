About This App

Vishal Insights is a fully independent, swadeshi digital learning platform created and developed by Vishal Surya.

The journey started as a simple website. Over time, it evolved into a structured multi-page knowledge ecosystem and now operates as a complete app-style platform.

The goal is simple: structured knowledge, professional presentation, and continuous innovation.

Platform Structure & Pages

1. Home Page – Central introduction of the platform, vision, and navigation to all sections.

2. Insights TV – A professional streaming-style video section where documentaries and educational videos are published. To submit video proposals, contact via email.

3. About Page – Detailed explanation of the platform vision and the creator.

4. Blogs Page – Educational articles, structured insights, and published thoughts. Blog submissions can be sent via email for review.

5. VIMAAN AI – An intelligent AI-based learning assistant designed to simplify complex topics and improve understanding.

6. Feedback Page – Users can rate, comment, and interact. Admin replies are supported.
